Research

Statistical methods development for phylogenetic trees

I work with Amy Willis to develop methods to compare and visualize phylogenetic trees. We are currently developing a visualization tool to help researchers choose which genes to include in an estimated consensus phylogeny.

Statistical modeling of wage heterogeneity

I work with Tyler McCormick to develop Bayesian hierarchical models for wage heterogeneity. We use longitudinal survey data to study variation in wages in low resource settings.
