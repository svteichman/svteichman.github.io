Research

Visualization of phylogenetic trees

I work with Amy Willis to develop methods to compare and visualize phylogenetic trees. We are currently developing a visualization tool to help researchers compare individual gene phylogenies in order to identify genes that have evolved differently and the effect of these outlying genes on an estimated genome level phylogenomic tree.

Statistical modeling of wage heterogeneity

I have worked with Tyler McCormick to develop a Bayesian hierarchical model to study wage heterogeneity. This project uses longitudinal survey data to study variation in wages in low resource settings.
